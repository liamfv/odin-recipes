Slow Cooker Italian Beef

Ingredients

  • 3 cups water
  • 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (5 pound) rump roast

Steps

  1. Combine water, salad dressing mix, oregano, basil, parsley, onion salt,
    garlic powder, bay leaf, salt, and black pepper in a saucepan.
    Stir well, and bring to a boil.
  2. Place rump roast in a slow cooker; pour hot herb mixture over the meat.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 10 to 12 hours, or on High for 4 to 5 hours.
    Remove bay leaf; shred beef with a fork and serve with some of the hot gravy.
